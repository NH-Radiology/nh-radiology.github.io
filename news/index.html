Legislative issues

The NHRS contributes expertise and opinions on many healthcare legislative issues in the state. A few recent “radiology’ focused issues include:

  • 2017 SB189 The society help support breast tomosynthesis coverage from private insurers
  • 2017 HB 184 The society help support the opposition to repeal of licensure for medical imaging and radiation professionals
